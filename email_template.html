INSTRUCTIONS FOR SENDING GIFT CARDS

There are two ways to send gift cards to the recipients. We strongly recommend the first option (e-gift cards) because it is fast and secure:

  1. Purchasing an electronic gift card (e-gift card) on the store website and having the store send the card by email to the recipient. This is the safest and quickest way to support people.
  2. Purchasing a physical gift card online and having the store send the card by postal mail to the recipient. This is secure, but does not get support to current EPA employees and SEE enrollees as quickly as we would hope.

We advise against purchasing a physical gift card in the store and mailing it to the recipient yourself. This method is unsafe due to recent fraudulent activity. There has been a rash of cases where thieves tampered with the card on display and then stole the funds before the intended recipient could cash it. You would be unaware that the card is useless to the recipient. If you must buy a card in a store, try to buy one that has been stored safely behind a counter rather than one that has been hanging on a rack.

Make sure you buy only a card usable in the store designated by the recipient, not a cash card which could be used in a variety of stores as cash. It would be illegal for the recipient to receive such a cash card from you.
